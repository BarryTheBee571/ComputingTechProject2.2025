Recipe 6

Coconut Curry Chickpeas

  • 1 yellow onion (A$0.55)
  • 2 cloves garlic (A$0.25)
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger (A$0.15)
  • 30ml olive oil (A$0.60)
  • 2 Tbsp curry powder (A$0.70)
  • 1 tsp cumin (A$0.15)
  • 2 x 400g cans chickpeas, drained (A$2.40)
  • 230g tomato sauce (A$0.75)
  • 400ml coconut milk (A$4.50)
  • 85g chopped kale (A$1.10)

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion, garlic, ginger in oil until soft. Add curry powder and cumin, cook 1 min.
  2. Add chickpeas, tomato sauce, coconut milk. Simmer 15 min.
  3. Add kale, cook until wilted. Season to taste. Serve with rice or bread.
