One Pot Creamy Pesto Chicken Pasta

  • 450g boneless, skinless chicken breast (A$9.40)
  • 30g butter (A$0.40)
  • 2 cloves garlic (A$0.25)
  • 225g penne pasta (A$1.00)
  • 350ml chicken broth (A$0.30)
  • 240ml milk (A$0.30)
  • 85g cream cheese (A$1.20)
  • 80g basil pesto (A$1.10)
  • 25g grated Parmesan (A$0.65)
  • Freshly cracked pepper (A$0.05)
  • Pinch crushed red pepper (A$0.03)
  • 90g fresh spinach (A$1.40, optional)
  • 35g sun dried tomatoes (A$1.70, optional)

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into 2.5cm pieces. Melt butter in a deep skillet, add chicken, and cook until browned.
  2. Add minced garlic, sauté 1 min. Add pasta and broth, bring to boil, cover, simmer 8 min, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add milk, cream cheese, and pesto. Stir until cheese melts. Add Parmesan and combine.
  4. Stir in spinach and sun dried tomatoes if using. Cook until spinach wilts. Season with pepper and red pepper. Serve.
