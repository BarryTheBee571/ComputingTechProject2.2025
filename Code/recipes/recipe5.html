Recipe 5

Meatballs

  • 60g plain breadcrumbs (A$0.25)
  • 60g grated Parmesan (A$2.90)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder (A$0.08)
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder (A$0.08)
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning (A$0.08)
  • 1/4 tsp salt (A$0.03)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper (A$0.05)
  • 2 large eggs (A$2.40)
  • 60ml whole milk (A$0.06)
  • 450g Italian sausage (A$5.40)
  • 450g ground beef (A$7.50)

Instructions

  1. Mix breadcrumbs, Parmesan, spices. Whisk eggs separately.
  2. Combine all ingredients, mix gently. Shape into 32 balls.
  3. Bake at 200°C for 15 min or pan-fry until browned and cooked through. Simmer in sauce if desired. Serve.
