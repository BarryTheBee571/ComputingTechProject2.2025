Recipe 7

Lo Mein

  • 170g noodles (A$2.80)
  • 3 Tbsp soy sauce (A$0.30)
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil (A$0.15)
  • 1 tsp sugar (A$0.03)
  • 1 tsp water
  • 30ml high heat cooking oil (A$0.15)
  • 3 cups mixed vegetables (A$6.10)

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles, drain. Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, water for sauce.
  2. Stir fry vegetables in hot oil 1 min. Add noodles and sauce, cook 2 min until sauce is absorbed. Serve.
