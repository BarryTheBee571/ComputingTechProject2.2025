Chicken Schnitzel

  • 2 large chicken breasts (780g, A$7.00)
  • 1 tsp salt (A$0.06)
  • 1 tsp black pepper (A$0.25)
  • 80g all-purpose flour (A$0.15)
  • 2 eggs (A$0.75)
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (A$0.06)
  • 1 tsp dried parsley (A$0.08)
  • 100g Panko breadcrumbs (A$1.25)
  • 65g Parmesan cheese (A$2.70)
  • 500ml vegetable oil (A$1.80)
  • 1 lemon, quartered (A$1.00)

Instructions

  1. Slice chicken breasts in half, pound to 6mm thick. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Set up 3 bowls: flour mix, egg wash, panko mix. Coat chicken in each, then place on tray.
  3. Heat oil to 175°C. Fry chicken 3-4 min per side until golden and cooked through. Drain, serve with lemon.
