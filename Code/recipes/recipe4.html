Slow Cooker Vegetarian Lentil Chili

  • 1 yellow onion (A$0.50)
  • 2 cloves garlic (A$0.25)
  • 400g can diced tomatoes (A$0.70)
  • 170g can tomato paste (A$0.70)
  • 400g can black beans, drained (A$0.90)
  • 400g can kidney beans, drained (A$0.90)
  • 200g brown lentils, uncooked (A$1.10)
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder (A$0.45)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika (A$0.15)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin (A$0.15)
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder (A$0.08)
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder (A$0.05)
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (A$0.05)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (A$0.03)
  • Black pepper (A$0.05)
  • 2 Tbsp coconut oil (A$0.20)
  • 950ml vegetable broth (A$0.80)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to slow cooker, stir to combine.
  2. Cook on high 4 hours (or low 8 hours) until lentils are tender. Season to taste and serve hot.
