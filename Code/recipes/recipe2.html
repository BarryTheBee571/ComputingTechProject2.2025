Mac and Cheese

  • 225g macaroni (A$0.75)
  • 45g butter (A$0.60)
  • 24g all-purpose flour (A$0.10)
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder (A$0.05)
  • 480ml whole milk (A$0.55)
  • 1/2 tsp hot sauce (A$0.03)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (A$0.02)
  • 225g sharp cheddar, shredded (A$3.40)

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni in boiling water for 8 min, drain.
  2. Melt butter in a pot, whisk in flour and onion powder, cook 1 min.
  3. Whisk in milk, simmer until thickened. Turn off heat, add salt and hot sauce.
  4. Gradually whisk in cheese until melted. Add pasta, stir to combine. Serve hot.
