Recipe 3

Chicken and Rice

  • 2 tsp paprika (A$0.30)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano (A$0.15)
  • 1 tsp dried thyme (A$0.15)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder (A$0.08)
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder (A$0.08)
  • 1/4 tsp salt (A$0.03)
  • 1/4 tsp pepper (A$0.03)
  • 570g boneless chicken thighs (A$7.00)
  • 30ml cooking oil (A$0.15)
  • 1 yellow onion, diced (A$0.50)
  • 200g long-grain white rice (A$0.50)
  • 420ml vegetable broth (A$0.35)
  • 1 Tbsp chopped parsley (A$0.15, optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix spices, coat chicken. Brown chicken in oil, set aside.
  2. Sauté onion, add rice, toast 2 min. Add broth, return chicken on top.
  3. Cover, boil, then simmer 20 min. Rest 5 min, fluff rice, garnish with parsley.
